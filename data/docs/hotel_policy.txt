GRAND PALACE HOTEL - POLICIES & PROCEDURES

CHECK-IN / CHECK-OUT
- Standard check-in time: 3:00 PM
- Standard check-out time: 12:00 PM (noon)
- Early check-in available from 12:00 PM subject to availability (fee: $30)
- Late check-out until 3:00 PM available for $40. After 3PM charged as full night.

CANCELLATION POLICY
- Free cancellation up to 48 hours before check-in
- Within 48 hours: first night charged as penalty
- No-show: full stay charged
- Non-refundable rates cannot be cancelled

ROOM TYPES
- Standard Room: Up to 2 guests, 28sqm, city view, base rate $120/night
- Deluxe Room: Up to 2 guests, 38sqm, garden or pool view, base rate $200/night
- Suite: Up to 4 guests, 65sqm, panoramic view, Jacuzzi, private balcony, base rate $380/night

LOYALTY PROGRAM
- Standard tier: 1 point per $1 spent
- Silver tier (10+ stays): 1.5x points, free breakfast
- Gold tier (25+ stays): 2x points, free breakfast + room upgrade

AMENITIES
- Pool: Open 7AM-10PM
- Spa: Open 9AM-9PM, advance booking recommended
- Restaurant: Breakfast 7-10:30AM, Lunch 12-3PM, Dinner 6:30-10:30PM
- Gym: Open 24 hours, key card access
- Business center: Open 8AM-8PM

PRICING STRATEGY
- Peak season (June-August, December): rates increase 20-30%
- Weekend premium: +15-20% on Friday and Saturday nights
- Last-minute (within 24hrs): dynamic pricing, may go up or down
- Minimum stay during holidays: 3 nights
