HOTEL OPERATIONS GUIDE

REVENUE MANAGEMENT
- RevPAR (Revenue Per Available Room) = Total Room Revenue / Total Available Rooms
- ADR (Average Daily Rate) = Total Room Revenue / Rooms Sold
- Occupancy Rate = Rooms Sold / Total Available Rooms x 100
- Target occupancy: 75%+ on weekdays, 90%+ on weekends
- Target RevPAR: $150+ on regular nights, $200+ on peak nights

PRICING RECOMMENDATIONS
- Occupancy >90%: Increase rates by 15-25%
- Occupancy 75-90%: Maintain or slight increase (5-10%)
- Occupancy 50-75%: Consider promotions or slight reduction
- Occupancy <50%: Activate discount packages, flash sales

HOUSEKEEPING STANDARDS
- Room turnover target: 30 minutes for standard, 45 for deluxe, 60 for suite
- Deep clean schedule: every 7 days per occupied room
- Linen change: every 3 days or on guest request
- Minibar restock: on each checkout

MAINTENANCE PROTOCOL
- Urgent issues (flooding, no AC, electrical): 30 minutes response
- High priority (TV, WiFi, plumbing minor): 2 hours response
- Standard (light bulbs, cosmetic): next business day

GUEST EXPERIENCE TARGETS
- Check-in time: under 5 minutes
- Guest satisfaction score target: 4.5/5
- Complaint resolution: within 2 hours
- Repeat guest rate target: 35%+
